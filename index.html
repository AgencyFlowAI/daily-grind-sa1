Our Story

A Little Place with a Lot of Heart

Tucked inside the Ethos Building on Kings Road, Daily Grind is a small, family-run cafe that does things properly. Every sandwich is made fresh, every cake is baked in-house, and the coffee is always hot.

Whether you're grabbing a quick breakfast before work or sitting down for a proper lunch, you'll find friendly faces, honest food, and fair prices.
